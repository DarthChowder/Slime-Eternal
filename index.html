Slime Eternal

Guide your slime from the Start to an Exit in as few moves as possible!

Controls:
- Click a tile to move
- Arrow Keys or WASD to move

Rules:
- You start with 3 moves
- Each move shrinks your slime
- Collect blobs for extra moves
- Avoid traps (lava) or die
- Grab coins for cosmetics
- Reach an exit to advance!
